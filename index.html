Work



















Generali France

Senior analyst•September 2014 - September 2018

As a member of the ALM & Strategic Asset Allocation team my missions were focused on the definition of investment strategies for the savings and P&L portfolios. Those activities involve various analysis on the portfolio liquidity, risks, competitiveness... It also include the optimisation of the portfolios under objectives and constraints, valuation of investment strategies and the analysis and implementation of derivatives.

One of the most important project I did was to develop and maintain an ALM projection tool for investment planning and ALM activities. The tool was built to perform a monthly projection of all assets, perform what if hypothesis on the asset characteristics and the liabilities and to project the portfolio with the asset / libilities interaction realizing sells and buys of assets simulating the realization of objective under constraints.

This position also included the necessity to communicate through presentations, reports and meetings to inform or beeing consulted on various subjects about the company situation and to support top management decisions. I also tutored interns, conduct & follow their activities and assist them on their master thesis.

BNP Paribas Cardif

Actuarial Intern• May 2014 - August 2014
